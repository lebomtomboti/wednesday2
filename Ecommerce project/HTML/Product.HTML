Cast iron kettlebell

The cast iron kettlebell is cast with solid bowels, so the bell size and handle width vary by weight. The larger the weight, the larger the bell diameter and the larger the handle width. The handle shape of the cast iron kettlebell is an arc, which is bent from the horn to the corner and gradually widens to the sides.
